Franklin is a dog. Franklin has several friends. His best friend is bear. He likes to play with rabbit and fox. They like to ride bicycles and play basketball together. 
Franklin and bear's favorite game to play is hide and go seek. One day when they were playing they came across their friend skunk and invited him to play. Skunk is not the best at playing hide and seek but she tries her best.
But, by accident, skunk farted. As it smell too bad, everyone could notice where the skunk is, but cannot reach to her. But, skunk did not even notice that she emitted the badly smelling gas.
